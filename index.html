FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Haas really free to start?+
Yes, completely. Create one résumé, get 10 job matches per day, and verify one document — all without paying a single rupee. Upgrade only when you need more.
How does AI document verification work?+
Upload a photo or scan of your certificate or ID. Our AI analyzes the document's authenticity markers and cross-references known formats. Verified documents earn a Haas Trust Badge visible to all employers on the platform.
Can I use Haas from a small town or village?+
Absolutely. Haas is built for every Indian job seeker — not just metro city residents. Our matching covers 500+ cities and villages, and we prioritize remote-friendly opportunities accessible to anyone.
Is my personal data safe with Haas?+
Your data is encrypted end-to-end, stored on secure servers, and never shared or sold to any third party. You control what's visible and can delete your entire account at any time.
Can I download my résumé as a PDF?+
Yes! Every plan — including Free — lets you preview your résumé in full screen and download it as a professionally formatted PDF. Premium users get access to 20+ template designs.
